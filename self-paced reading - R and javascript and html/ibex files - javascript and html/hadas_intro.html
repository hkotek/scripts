Experiment Instructions

In order to participate in this experiment, you need to be a native speaker of American English (i.e. you must have moved to the U.S. before the age of 6 and you must have lived there most of your life).

You will be asked to read a number of English sentences one word at a time. You can advance to the next word by pressing the space bar. You should try to read the sentences at a natural pace, making sure that you understand what you read.

Each sentence will be followed by content a question about that sentence. Try to answer the questions as accurately as possible given the content of the most recently displayed sentence. Press 1 or 2 to choose the correct answer.

Feedback about the experiment is always welcome. Thank you for helping us understand the cognitive processes involved in reading.
